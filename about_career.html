Our Career

TP Apparel is an African dynamic conglomerate company that encompasses an array of distinctive ventures, each contributing to its overarching mission of becoming the ultimate house of luxury, defining style and creating a compelling experience.

Our Potential

Timmy Patrick, the CEO of TP Apparel, created an avenue to tackle the rise of counterfeit pieces in Nigeria, which pose a threat to the integrity and profitability of the luxury retail industry, by getting accreditation directly from the high-fashion brands.

Our Value

Today TP Apparel connects fashion lovers in over 50 countries and territories with over 100 of the world's best brands. Our values and guiding principles are centered around our employees, innovation, customer focus, collaboration and accountability.

Our Mission

The ultimate house of luxury, defining style, creating a compelling experience. Every interaction with our brand should leave a lasting impression, inspiring awe and delight in our guests and fostering a sense of belonging to an exclusive community of discerning individuals.

Our Vision

To become Africa's number one destination for luxury, this journey begins with a meticulous focus on quality across every aspect of the customer experience. Attention to detail is paramount, ensuring that every interaction with the brand reflects excellence and sophistication.
Timmy Patrick Apparel is a premier e-commerce destination where style meets luxery, renowned for providing its customers with specially curated high-fashion and cutting-edge designer cloths.
